The thirty-second takeaway

Llama can give a confident-looking legal answer while a linear probe over its hidden activations assigns very different scores to individual claims. The paper result is not that this probe is a legal oracle. It is that hidden states carry claim-level correctness signal that the model's own self-report mostly fails to expose.

Static artifact. The page loads precomputed examples from examples.json. It does not run live generation, call a model server, or operate a legal QA product.
